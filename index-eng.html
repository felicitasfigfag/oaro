"OLEA Genesis".Animated 3D sculpture. FHD video (1920 x 1080). Duration 0:59 sec. NFT (Non Fungible Token - Non Fungible Token).

The work will be delivered in digital Token and Pen Drive.

OLEA Genesis corresponds to the first unit of a cryptocurrency (OLEA) programmed by the artist and sold in the form of digital works certified as NFTs (Non Fungible Tokens).

This video shows us the representation of this cryptographic unit that has an extension in the natural world since the code of this cryptocurrency is also stored in DNA (genetic code) and encapsulated in olive oil.

With this project, the artist has managed to connect in a biological way, the economies of agriculture and raw materials together with the new economies related to the internet and blockchain, achieving an artistic project that is unique in the world.

With the purchase of the artwork, the buyer also obtains the OLEA token within the blockchain, which means an added value to this purchase.

ABOUT THE ARTIST

Spanish artist who resides between France and Spain. His main artistic activity focuses on the interpretation of social, ethical, moral, psychological, economic, sociological and artistic changes derived from the digital revolution and the application of new technologies in the sphere of the common.

Solimán also directs the Innovation laboratory at the School of Art and Technology of Valencia and is the founder of the Harddiskmuseum in 2014, the first Museum of the history of art that lives on a 2TB hard drive full of digital art works of more of 125 international artists. This Museum has also been the first in history to have a "backup" of its metadata stored in genetic code.

Solimán has exhibited his work internationally in several countries, highlighting his participation in the Digital Art Biennale in Brazil, the ZKM in Karlsruhe in Germany, the Asia Center in Gwanyu in South Korea, the Ars Electronica Festival in Linz in Austria, or the Los Angeles Codame Festival among others.

He is currently investigating the biological relationships between blockchains and natural liquids such as olive oil. The OLEA project addresses these issues and will soon be presented at the Kiev Digital Art Biennale in Ukraine, the Share Festival in Turin, Italy or the Karachi Biennial in Pakistan.

At ARCO 2021 , Solimán López was the first artist to sell an NFT in the context of a fair.
